## 1. How does effective communication reduce project risks?
Effective communication acts as an early warning system for risks. In our "SKIPZ" project, we identified "Data Privacy" as a key risk. By explicitly listing "Data Subjects" as stakeholders in our communication plan, we ensure that ethical considerations aren't forgotten during development. Furthermore, regular updates to the University Lab Admin ensure we don't face unexpected infrastructure blocks (like network bans) that could delay our model training. Clear communication ensures everyone knows *who* is responsible for *what*, preventing tasks from being dropped.
